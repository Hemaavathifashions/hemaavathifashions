Our Policies

Shipping Policy

We aim to process and ship all orders within 1-2 business days. Delivery times vary based on location:

  • Chennai: 1-2 days
  • Tamil Nadu: 2-4 days
  • Rest of India: 3-7 days

Return & Exchange Policy

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for store credit or exchange. Items must be:

  • Unwashed and unworn
  • With original tags attached
  • In original packaging

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Any personal information collected will be:

  • Used only for order processing and communication
  • Never shared with third parties without consent
  • Securely stored and protected

Quality Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our products. If you receive a defective item, we'll replace it at no additional cost.
