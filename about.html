About Hemaavathi Fashions

Welcome to Hemaavathi Fashions, your destination for stylish, affordable fashion for the whole family.

Our Story

Established in the early 2010s in Kowlepalli, Kadiri, we've grown from a small family shop to a trusted name in the Sri Sathya Sai District for both tradition and modern trends.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, fashionable clothing at prices you’ll love—serving thousands of families with curated sarees, dresses, men’s and kids’ wear, plus handlooms and more.

Our Values

  • Quality craftsmanship
  • Ethical sourcing
  • Customer satisfaction
  • Community support
