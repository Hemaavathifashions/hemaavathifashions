Shipping Policy

We aim to process and ship all orders within 1–2 business days. Delivery times vary based on location:

  • Rest of India: 3–7 days

Return & Exchange Policy

No returns unless the item purchased in store is clearly damaged—we accept returns within 30 days for such cases.

Quality Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our products. If you receive a defective item, we'll replace it at no additional cost.
